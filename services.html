Concept

The design process begins with getting to know your lifestyle and needs through a detailed conversation and analysis. We visit the site, study the space, and develop thoughtful planning solutions. Based on these, we shape the interior concept - combining mood, materials, and style into a cohesive vision, presented through curated references and moodboards.

Visualisation

At this stage, the design comes to life. We create high-quality, photorealistic 3D images that allow you to see exactly how your future space will look - down to the lighting, textures, and ambiance.

Documentation

We develop a full set of construction drawings to ensure precise project execution. These include every stage of the build, from electrical and plumbing plans to the calculation of all finishing materials. Custom drawings are created for built-in and made-to-measure furniture. A detailed specification lists all materials, fixtures, and furniture - with accurate quantities, real-time pricing, and direct links to manufacturers or stores.

Procurement

Procurement is a well-structured process of selecting, purchasing, and coordinating the delivery of all finishes and interior elements for project implementation. We source furniture, materials, lighting, and décor, liaise directly with suppliers, manage logistics, and ensure quality control. Through established partnerships, you gain access to exclusive discounts and considerable cost savings.

Autor's Supervision

Comprehensive support throughout the project implementation. This includes regular site visits to monitor progress, communicate tasks to contractors, and ensure accurate execution according to the final design. We stay in constant contact with all parties involved contractors, specialists, and suppliers and make adjustments to drawings when needed.
